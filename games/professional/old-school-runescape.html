About Old School Runescape

Jagex | Proprietary Engine | Jira

Old School RuneScape is one of the world's largest and most popular MMORPG'S. It features a persistent world in which players can interact with each other and the environment. This is the open world you know and love, but as it was in 2007.

I worked as an intern on the engine team to support the operation and on-going enhancement of the RS engine and associated systems.

My Contributions

  • Enhanced engine systems and contributed to the Steam client
  • Used C++ for client-side development and optimization
  • Developed a title screen volume slider from scratch, handling sprite rendering and data persistence
  • Gained insight into engine programming , including how it differs from gameplay programming
  • Modified in-game behaviour using RuneScript
  • Gained experience with using Git commands with Git Bash
  • Participated in code reviews and sprint planning
  • Gained firsthand experience working on a live MMORPG
